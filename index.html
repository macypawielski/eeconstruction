Welcome to Engage Events –



Your dedicated partner in crafting unforgettable weddings throughout Santa Fe and its surrounding areas. Founded with a passion for creating moments that last a lifetime, Engage Events is your go-to wedding planning team for a seamless and personalized experience. In the enchanting landscapes of Northern New Mexico, our team, led by Anna Kaiser, brings a wealth of experience to ensure your wedding day is nothing short of magical.

Our approach is simple: we listen to your vision and transform it into a beautifully curated celebration that reflects your unique love story. From the historic charm of Santa Fe to the breathtaking landscapes of the surrounding areas, we specialize in creating weddings that capture the essence of the Southwest.

At Engage Events, we believe in the power of details. Whether it's selecting the perfect venue, coordinating vendors, or adding personalized DIY touches, our team is committed to ensuring that your wedding day is stress-free and filled with joy. Let us be your guide as you embark on this exciting journey.

Engage Events is here to make your dream wedding a reality, leaving you free to savor every moment of your special day. Experience the simplicity of perfect weddings with Engage Events – contact us today!
